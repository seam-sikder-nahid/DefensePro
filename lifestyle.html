üí™ Physical Fitness Standards

Running

5 km Run: 24 minutes (Male), 28 minutes (Female)

How to prepare: Start with 2-3 km daily, gradually increase distance. Run at steady pace, 4-5 days per week.

Push-ups

Target: 20 Push-ups in 2 minutes

How to prepare: Start with 5-10 daily. Do 3 sets of maximum reps. Rest 48 hours between sessions.

Sit-ups

Target: 10 Sit-ups in 10 seconds

How to prepare: Daily practice of core strengthening. Do planks, crunches, and leg raises.

Pull-ups

Target: 10 Pull-ups minimum

How to prepare: Use assisted pull-up machine, then progressive resistance training. 3 sessions/week.

üò¥ Sleep & Rest Schedule

Recommended Sleep Duration

üõèÔ∏è 7-8 hours of quality sleep per night

Sleep is crucial for memory consolidation and physical recovery. Don't compromise on sleep.

Sleep Hygiene Tips

  • Go to bed and wake up at the same time daily
  • Keep bedroom dark, cool, and quiet
  • Avoid screens 1 hour before sleep
  • No caffeine after 3 PM
  • Light exercise 3-4 hours before sleep
  • Practice relaxation techniques (deep breathing, meditation)

Weekly Rest Day

üí™ Take 1 complete rest day per week for recovery. Light activities like walking or yoga are okay.

üß† Mental Health & Stress Management

Meditation & Mindfulness

Practice 10-15 minutes daily. Focus on breathing. Helps reduce anxiety and improves concentration.

Yoga

20-30 minutes, 3-4 times per week. Improves flexibility, balance, and mental clarity.

Social Connection

Talk to family and friends. Don't isolate yourself. Maintain healthy relationships.

Hobby & Recreation

Engage in activities you enjoy. Read, music, sports, art. Balance study with leisure.

Positive Self-Talk

Believe in yourself. Celebrate small wins. Don't let failures discourage you.

Seek Professional Help

If struggling with anxiety or depression, consult a mental health professional. It's okay to ask for help.

‚è±Ô∏è Time Management Tips

1

Create a Study Schedule

Plan your study hours based on your peak performance time. Most people are productive in morning hours.

2

Use the Pomodoro Technique

Study for 25 minutes intensely, then take 5-minute breaks. After 4 cycles, take a 15-minute break.

3

Prioritize Important Tasks

Identify high-impact topics. Focus on difficult subjects when you're fresh and alert.

4

Eliminate Distractions

Put phone on silent, use website blockers, find a quiet study space. Create a distraction-free environment.

5

Track Your Progress

Keep a daily log. Review completed topics. This boosts motivation and shows your improvement.

6

Learn to Say No

Don't overcommit. It's okay to decline social events if you need to study. Balance is important though.
